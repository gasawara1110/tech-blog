# AGENTS.md

## Project Overview

This repository manages technical writing assets for robotics-related engineering output.

The main goal is to accumulate technical credibility under the author's own name as an integrated robotics engineer who can conceptualize, design, implement, and validate robot systems.

## Primary Language Policy

- Japanese is the primary writing language.
- English articles are translations or adapted versions of the Japanese source articles.
- Do not create an English article without a corresponding Japanese source article unless explicitly instructed.
- When translating, preserve the technical meaning rather than translating word-for-word.
- Keep technical terms consistent with `docs/terminology.md`.
